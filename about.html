The Studio

Born from a Passion for Cinema.

Tumbakaar Studios was built on a simple belief—that every story deserves to be told beautifully. Whether it is the dynamic pacing of a social reel, the warmth of a family brand story, or a moving cinematic event film... we treat every project as a work of art.

The name Tumbakaar symbolizes the spirit of craftspeople who mold, shape, refine, and bring ideas into raw physical existence. That is exactly what we do with every frame we touch.

From a dynamic wedding film that makes you cry to high-end brand creatives, our studio is a one-stop creative powerhouse built for the modern age, serving clients across India, entirely remotely.

The Founder

Shubhanshu

Founder & Premium Editor — Tumbakaar Studios

A graduate of AAFT (Asian Academy of Film & Television) — one of India's most prestigious media institutions — Shubhanshu brings over 3 years of hands-on industry experience to every single project.

He founded Tumbakaar Studios to bring film-quality craft, precision color grading, and high-retention post-production to everyday creators, couples, and brands across India. Shubhanshu is dedicated to turning raw footage into cinematic assets that captivate audiences and leave a lasting impact.
