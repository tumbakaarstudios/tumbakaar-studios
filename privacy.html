Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Tumbakaar Studio ("we," "us," "our," or the "Studio") operates the website tumbakaar.com (the "Site") and provides bespoke digital content creation services, including but not limited to, high-fidelity video editing, animation, visual effects, and digital e-invitations (collectively, the "Services").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data and proprietary media assets you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, processing, usage, disclosure, and safeguarding of your information in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 (India), and other applicable global data protection regulations.

By accessing our Site or engaging our Services, you explicitly consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver professional-grade creative services, we collect information that falls into the following categories:

A. Information Voluntarily Provided by You

  • Identity & Contact Data: Full name, business/organization name, email address, telephone/WhatsApp number, and billing address.
  • Client-Furnished Media & Project Assets: Raw video footage, imagery, photographs, audio files, scripts, branding assets (logos, fonts), and specific textual details (e.g., event dates, venue locations, host names) provided for the execution of your project.
  • Financial Data: Billing information, tax identifiers (such as GSTIN, if applicable), and transaction records.

B. Automatically Collected Data (Technical & Usage Information)

  • Device & Log Data: Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types, operating system, and platform.
  • Analytical Data: Details of your visits to our Site, traffic data, location data, and user interaction metrics captured via cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2. Legal Basis and Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data strictly under valid legal grounds—primarily for the performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, or based on your explicit consent. The purposes include:

  • Service Delivery: To execute, render, and deliver custom animations, video edits, and e-invite assets as per project specifications.
  • Client Management: To process transactions, manage billing, issue invoices, and maintain robust customer support communications.
  • Quality Control & Optimization: To internalize feedback, improve website functionality, and optimize user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To prevent fraudulent transactions and comply with statutory tax laws or government directives.

3. Intellectual Property, Portfolio, and Promotional Usage

Given the creative nature of our Studio, we actively showcase our design capabilities.

  • Portfolio Rights: Unless otherwise explicitly agreed upon in a written contract prior to project commencement, Tumbakaar Studio retains the right to display fragments, summaries, or the final render of the commissioned digital assets (including e-invites and animations) on our website, social media channels (e.g., Instagram), and professional showreels for promotional and portfolio purposes.
  • Privacy Opt-Out: We understand that certain projects contain sensitive personal information (such as private wedding venues or personal phone numbers). You possess the right to restrict public portfolio usage. To request non-disclosure, you must submit a formal written request to tumbakaarstudios@gmail.com prior to the final project delivery.

4. Data Retention Policy

  • Personal Data: We retain your identity and billing data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or to fulfill legal, tax, and accounting mandates.
  • Project Files and Raw Assets: Due to storage constraints and privacy standards, raw assets and project files (such as Adobe Premiere/After Effects source files) are archived for a maximum of 30 days post final project delivery and client approval. After this period, raw media is permanently purged from our storage arrays. Final deliverables may be archived longer for portfolio continuity.

5. Third-Party Disclosures and Transfers

Tumbakaar Studio does not sell, lease, or trade your personal information or media assets to third parties. Data sharing is strictly restricted to trusted entities necessary for operations:

  • Service Providers: Third-party cloud storage, secure payment gateways, and software infrastructure providers who operate under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your data if mandated to do so by law enforcement, regulatory bodies, or judiciary courts to protect our legal rights or ensure public safety.

6. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to secure your personal data and creative media against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure cloud servers, restricted access protocols, and encrypted communication channels. However, please be advised that no digital transmission or electronic storage method can guarantee absolute, infallible security.

7. Your Statutory Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including the DPDP Act, 2023), you hold distinct rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access and Correction: You can request a summary of your personal data processed by us and ask for corrections if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request the deletion of your personal data, provided it is no longer required for legal compliance or contractual execution.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent for data processing at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to the withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our designated Grievance/Privacy Officer at the email provided below.

Your continued interaction with our Site or utilization of our Services post-update constitutes your formal acceptance of the revised policy.
